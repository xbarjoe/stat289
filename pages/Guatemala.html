The Republic of Guatemala is a nation of Central America . Guatemala was freed of Spanish colonial rule in 1821 . During the second half of the 20th century , the nation experienced a variety of military and civilian governments as well as a 36-year guerrilla war. In 1996 , the government signed a peace agreement formally ending the conflict, which had led to the death of more than 100,000 people and had created some 1 million refugees.
