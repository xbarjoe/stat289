Honduras ( Spanish : República de Honduras , pronounced  [reˈpuβlika ðe onˈduɾas] ) is a republic in Central America . It was formerly known as Spanish Honduras to differentiate it from British Honduras (now Belize ). [4] The country is bordered to the west by Guatemala , to the southwest by El Salvador , to the southeast by Nicaragua , to the south by the Pacific Ocean at the Gulf of Fonseca , and to the north by the Gulf of Honduras , a large inlet of the Caribbean Sea . Its size is just over 112,000 km² with an estimated population of almost 8,000,000. Its capital is Tegucigalpa . About a third of the population of the country live on less than US$ 2 per day. [5]
