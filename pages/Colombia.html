Colombia , officially the Republic of Colombia (Spanish: República de Colombia ), is a unitary constitutional republic comprising thirty-two departments . The country is located in northwestern South America , bordered to the east by Venezuela [8] and Brazil ; [9] to the south by Ecuador and Peru ; [10] to the north by the Caribbean Sea ; to the northwest by Panama ; and to the west by the Pacific Ocean . Colombia has maritime borders with Venezuela , Jamaica , Haiti , the Dominican Republic , Honduras , Nicaragua and Costa Rica . [11] [12] Colombia is the 26th largest country by area and the 27th largest by population, and the third largest in South America after Brazil and Argentina. With over 46 million people, Colombia has the second largest population of any Spanish-speaking country in the world, after Mexico . It is also the only country in South America having coasts in the Pacific Ocean and in the Atlantic Ocean . Colombia is a middle power , with the fourth largest economy in Latin America , and the third largest in South America .
